Traveling Salesman

Welcome! Click on the map to select some stops for your trip. Or you can just pick one of the preset routes if you'd rather. When you have all the stops you want in your route click optimize and watch it find (one of) the shortest routes. The Traveling Salesman is a classic computer science problem. This app uses an algorithm which implements a simulated annealing strategy to quickly find one of the shortest routes for you. Give it a try!
